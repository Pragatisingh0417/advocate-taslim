Documents Required for Court Marriage

To proceed with a court marriage, both parties must provide the following documents:

Court Marriage Procedure in Ghaziabad

  • 1. Filing Notice: The couple must file a notice of their intention to marry before the marriage officer.
  • 2. Publication of Notice: The notice is published publicly for 30 day
  • 3. Objection Period: Any objections must be raised within this period.
  • 4. Verification: The officer verifies all documents and identities.
  • 5. Marriage Registration: After the notice period, the couple and witnesses appear before the officer to sign the declaration.
