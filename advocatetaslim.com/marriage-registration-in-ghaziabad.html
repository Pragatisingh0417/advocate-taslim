Marriage Registration

Marriage is a sacred bond, and registering it legally is the first step toward securing your relationship in the eyes of the law. Why Marriage Registration is Important

Marriage registration is not just a legal requirement—it serves as official proof of your relationship. The registered marriage certificate is essential for various purposes like

  • Applying for a passport or visa
  • Adding a spouse’s name to official documents
  • Claiming insurance or inheritance rights application
  • Resolving legal disputes related to marriage

With Advocate Taslim, you get professional assistance in completing all formalities under the Hindu Marriage Act, 1955, Special Marriage Act, 1954, or other applicable laws.

Documents Required for Marriage Registration
  • Proof of age (Birth Certificate, Passport, School Certificate, etc.)
  • Proof of residence (Aadhar Card, Voter ID, Driving License, etc.)
  • Passport-size photographs of both partners
  • Marriage invitation card (if applicable)
  • Witnesses’ ID proof and photographs
  • Affidavits declaring marital status, date of birth, and nationality
  • Witnesses with valid ID proofs
